Explore the famous cities of Odisha

Popular Cities in Odisha

Bhubaneswar, Odisha

The capital city known as the Temple City of India, famous for its ancient temples like Lingaraj Temple and vibrant cultural heritage.

Puri, Odisha

A coastal city renowned for the Jagannath Temple and its beautiful golden beaches attracting thousands of pilgrims and tourists.

Cuttack, Odisha

Known as the Silver City, Cuttack is famous for its intricate silver filigree work and rich cultural heritage.

Rourkela, Odisha

An industrial city known for the Rourkela Steel Plant and picturesque natural surroundings including hills and rivers.

Sambalpur, Odisha

Famous for the Hirakud Dam, Sambalpuri sarees, and rich tribal culture. A major hub of art and tradition.

Berhampur, Odisha

Popularly known as the Silk City, Berhampur is famous for its silk sarees, delicious seafood, and cultural heritage.

Popular Mountains

Mahendragiri
Mahendragiri, Gajapati
  •  350 reviews

Mahendragiri, the second-highest peak in Odisha, offers stunning views and rich mythology associated with the Pandavas.

Daringbadi
Daringbadi, Kandhamal
  •  400 reviews

Known as the ‘Kashmir of Odisha’, Daringbadi is a hill station offering beautiful valleys, coffee gardens, and pine forests.

Deomali
Deomali, Koraput
  •  450 reviews

The highest peak in Odisha, Deomali offers adventure trekking, panoramic views, and scenic beauty.

Niyamgiri
Niyamgiri Hills, Rayagada
  •  300 reviews

Niyamgiri Hills are known for their rich biodiversity and cultural significance to the Dongria Kondh tribe.

Gandhamardhan
Gandhamardhan Hills, Bargarh/Balangir
  •  320 reviews

A place of natural beauty and mythological importance, Gandhamardhan Hills offer trekking and medicinal plant research opportunities.

Malayagiri
Malayagiri, Angul
  •  280 reviews

Malayagiri, the third highest peak in Odisha, is popular for its lush greenery and thrilling trekking trails.

Simlipal
Simlipal Hills, Mayurbhanj
  •  360 reviews

Home to the famous Simlipal National Park, these hills offer diverse wildlife, beautiful waterfalls, and dense forests.

Satkosia
Satkosia Gorge, Angul
  •  310 reviews

Satkosia Gorge is a picturesque spot surrounded by hills, known for its beautiful river views and rich wildlife.
